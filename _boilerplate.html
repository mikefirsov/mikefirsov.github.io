Designing a marketplace for smarter stone, soil and filler material management

Client

NCC / Loop Rocks

My Role

UX Design, Research, Interaction Design, Prototyping, Usability Testing, Visual Design

Challenge

Loop Rocks is a marketplace app for smarter stone, soil and filler material management. The app helps construction companies reduce costs and environmental impact by reusing materials instead of buying new ones.

From the analytics we knew that even though users were creating plenty of ads, there were not enough deals happening inside the app. Which in turn lead to missed opportunities to help users and direct revenue loss.

One of the difficulties was the breadth of the target audience for the product. The team has been targeting owners of the small construction companies, construction site managers at medium-large sized companies and even private users.
